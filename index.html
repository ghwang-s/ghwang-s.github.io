About

I am an M.S. student in Computer Science and Technology at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. My research focuses on building efficient and reliable learning systems, with particular interests in knowledge distillation and machine learning.

Previously, I received my B.S. in Computer Science and Technology from Jiangxi University of Finance and Economics in 2025.
